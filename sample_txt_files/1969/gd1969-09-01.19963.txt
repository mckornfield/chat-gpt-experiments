notes:
- this source differs from the most recent previously circulating copy 
  (http://db.etree.org/shninfo_detail.php?shnid=5216) in that:
a). Casey Jones is not clipped
b). The Yellow Dog Story which appears before Dark Star is not on this tape. The transition 
    from the end of Easy Wind to the start of Dark Star on this tape is continuous.  This 
    would suggest that the Yellow Dog Story on the other source may not be from this date.
c). The version of Lovelight on this source is complete (~24 minutes) except for a brief gap.  
    The previous source cut after ~ 9 minutes
